Current status: Group stage matchday 2 is complete (through June 21). Matchday 3 runs June 22–27. The Round of 32 begins June 29.
⚠️ New Tiebreaker Rule — Head-to-Head! For the first time in World Cup history, FIFA is using head-to-head as the primary tiebreaker instead of goal difference.

Full order:
  1. Head-to-head points (mini-league if 3+ teams tied)
  2. Head-to-head goal difference
  3. Head-to-head goals scored
  4. Overall group goal difference
  5. Overall group goals scored
  6. Fair play points (yellow −1, indirect red −3, straight red −4, yellow+red −5)
  7. FIFA World Ranking

Impact so far: 🇲🇽 Mexico, 🇺🇸 USA, and 🇩🇪 Germany have all won their groups with a game to spare. 🇭🇹 Haiti, 🇹🇷 Türkiye, and 🇹🇳 Tunisia are eliminated.
